How to play

The goal of the game is to clear the most amount of rows possible until you can't keep up with the falling bricks. Try your best to get the highest score and buy custom skins to make your game experience a lot more fun!

Controls

  • Slide finger across the game board to move the falling piece left and right respectively
  • Tap the left side of the canvas to rotate the piece left and the right side of the canvas to rotate the piece to the right
  • Swipe finger down to hard drop the piece
  • Tap on the control box to swap in the current piece being held
